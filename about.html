Meet Maggie!

Hi, thats ME! An Atlanta based Graphic Designer gone UX/UI. I find happiness in being able to tell a story through simple designs with a little added personality. I love having the freedom to inspire through designs as they can create a vision for a brand, persuade someone to take an action or even bring a sense of feeling to the user.

In my free time you'll find me with a hot coffee in hand during the mornings and an ice cold margarita at night as I live for creating community with my people. You can catch me at a weddings creating the bridal parties makeup look. I continue to be in awe with the color palettes that coincide with makeup artistry.

I’d love to get to know you! Lets Connect!
