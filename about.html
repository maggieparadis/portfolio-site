Meet Maggie!

I’m Maggie (not Margret) just Maggie, but my friends call me Mags!
I’m an Atlanta based graphic designer gone ui designer. I find happiness in being able to tell a story through simple designs with a little added personality. I love having the freedom to create and inspire through designs as they can create a vision for a company, provide a safe environment, or even persuade someone to take an action.

In my free time you can catch me at a wedding creating the Bride’s makeup as I love the color palettes that coincide with makeup artistry. I live for hot coffee in the mornings , an ice cold margarita at night, creating community with my gang, and experiencing new things.

I’d love to get to know you! Lets Connect!
